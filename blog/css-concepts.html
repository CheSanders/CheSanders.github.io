What does it mean to display inline vs using inline block? Inline elements: -Respect left & right margins and padding, but not top & bottom -Cannot have a width and height set -Allow other elements to sit to their left and right. Block elements: -Respect all everything listed above -Force a line break after the block element Inline-block elements: -Allow other elements to sit to their left and right -Respect top & bottom margins and padding -Respect height and width
